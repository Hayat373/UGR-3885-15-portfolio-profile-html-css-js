Place Visited

Semen Mountain, located in Ethiopia's Semien Mountains National Park, is renowned for its stunning landscapes and rich biodiversity. This UNESCO World Heritage Site features dramatic cliffs, deep valleys, and unique rock formations, making it a popular destination for trekking and wildlife observation.

Afar Dalol, located in the northeastern part of Ethiopia, is a striking geological marvel renowned for its otherworldly landscapes and extreme climate. This region is characterized by colorful hydrothermal features, including salt flats, mineral deposits, and bubbling hot springs, resulting from volcanic activity.

Hagia Sophia, or Ayasofya, is an architectural masterpiece located in Istanbul, Turkey. Hagia Sophia show case rich history and stunning mosaics. Renowned for its massive dome, intricate mosaics, and historical significance, Hagia Sophia stands as a symbol of the confluence of cultures and religions, attracting millions of tourists and scholars each year.
